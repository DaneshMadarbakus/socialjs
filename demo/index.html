Social JS is a 4kb script for easy custom social share buttons.

I wanted to make custom social share buttons that can be implemented without writing line after line with JavaScript. This is very easy to implement and requires close to no skills in JavaScript.

The share buttons below are an example on how it could look. But you can customize the design to your liking. That's the real power of Social JS. 100% design control!
